Comercializadora MTD S.A. de C.V. is a company dedicated to brand importation and management, established in 2016 in Monterrey, Mexico. We focus on distributing innovative, high-quality products, building strong relationships with our business partners through exceptional customer service, reliable delivery times, and affordable prices. We have a distribution network that spans over 1,500 retailers nationwide. We help position carefully selected international brands with high potential in the Mexican market, thanks to our deep understanding of the market and consumer preferences. Our in-house team specializes in marketing and brand building, supporting our partners in reaching high-quality retailers and achieving success in Mexico. For brand owners, we guarantee increased brand awareness and value, sales growth, and a trust-based relationship founded on mutual understanding.

MISSION

Connect Mexican consumers with innovative and high-quality international brands in cosmetics and personal care through exceptional distribution, marketing, and brand management services. We are committed to creating long-lasting partnerships based on trust, performance, and a deep understanding of the market.

VISION

To be the leading gateway in Mexico for global beauty and personal care brands, recognized for our excellence in brand building, nationwide reach, and ability to turn emerging products into consumer-recognized brands.
